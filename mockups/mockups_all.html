Licenses are signed with Ed25519 by the vendor's private key and verified offline by Meridian using an embedded public key. The signed payload includes your customer ID, hardware fingerprint, bound domain, issuance + expiry dates, and a one-time nonce — so licenses cannot be forged, guessed, shared, reused, or extended.
