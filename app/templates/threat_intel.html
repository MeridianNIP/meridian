CISA's Known Exploited Vulnerabilities catalog — the federal remediation mandate list. If a CVE is here, it is being actively exploited and patching is urgent. Public JSON feed, no key. Catalog cached for 6 hours.

FIRST.org's Exploit Prediction Scoring System (EPSS) assigns a 0–1 probability of exploitation in the next 30 days, plus a percentile rank. Pair with CVSS to prioritise patching. Public API, no key.
